Tartar Sauce

From: Graziella

Description

This homemade tartar sauce is far more flavorful than the version found in restaurants. Serve with fish or other dishes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup good mayonnaise
  • 1/2 a small yellow onion
  • Fresh parsley
  • 1 medium-sized lactic acid pickle (the taste of vinegar-pickled pickles will not be as good in finished sauce)
  • 1-2 Tbls. fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Roughly chop parsley
  2. Finely dice onion and pickle
  3. Mix with mayo and lemon juice
  4. Season with salt and pepper
  5. Enjoy!
